Meet the Monsters

Sasquatch (Bigfoot)

Often called Bigfoot, is a legendary ape-like creature said to inhabit remote forests of North America, towering in size and cloaked in mystery. For generations, sightings, footprints, and folklore have fueled belief in this elusive giant, making it one of the most iconic cryptids in modern mythology.

Loch Ness Monster

The Loch Ness Monster, often called “Nessie,” is a legendary creature said to inhabit the deep, cold waters of Scotlands Loch Ness. Described as a long-necked, aquatic figure, Nessie has become one of the worlds most famous cryptids, inspiring decades of sightings, speculation, and mystery.

chupacabra

The Chupacabra is a modern cryptid born from reports of mysterious livestock attacks, described as a vampiric creature that drains animals of their blood. Emerging in the 1990s across Puerto Rico and the Americas, it has since become a symbol of rural fear, unexplained sightings, and the power of rumor to shape monsters.

Mothman

The Mothman is a mysterious winged creature reportedly seen in Point Pleasant, West Virginia, during the late 1960s, often described as having glowing red eyes and a towering, humanoid shape. Considered an omen of disaster by some and a misunderstood cryptid by others, it remains one of Americas most enduring modern legends.
